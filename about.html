About Our Mission & Heritage

Preserving generations of classic milestones, developmental pipelines, and the cultural pulse points of Philippine Basketball.

The Roots of the Philippine Obsession

In the Philippines, basketball is far more than a sporting event—it is an inherited cultural cornerstone. From the makeshift wooden hoops nailed to coconut trees in rural barangays to the packed, roaring coliseums of metropolitan Manila, the game serves as a shared language across generations. This registry was founded to ensure that the rich histories, legendary player metrics, and foundational corporate leagues that paved the way for modern Philippine basketball are never forgotten.

Our historical archive meticulously chronicles the evolution of local basketball structures. We analyze how regional grassroots tournaments, collegiate athletic programs, and industrial farm setups historically intersected to create world-class athletes. By documenting the journeys of past icons and active icons alike, we provide sports historians, scouts, and fans with an immutable record of our national passion.

The National Passion

Tracking the sport's deep cultural penetration from raw amateur community spaces up to major global professional stages.

Scope of Records

Documenting precise historical player metrics, classic corporate farm leagues, team lineages, and institutional milestones.

Career Tracking

Traces the active journeys of local icons serving across the NBA, PBA, CESAFI, NCAA, UAAP, or international coaching ranks.

Our Archival Pillars

1. Historical Integrity

We corroborate player statistics, tournament score sheets, and roster changes through old newsprint archives and verified athletic records to maintain a precise repository.

2. Regional Inclusion

Our focus stretches beyond Manila. We actively research and document Southern basketball networks, Cebuano dynasties, and Mindanao regional standouts who left an uncredited mark.

3. Preserving the Pipeline

By showcasing the historical connection between corporate farm systems and collegiate developments, we educate the public on how elite athletes are structured.
